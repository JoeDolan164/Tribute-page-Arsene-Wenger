Arsene Wenger

Former manager of Arsenal Football Club

Here are some of the achievements of Arsene Wenger:

  • Premier League Title (1997-98) - Wenger led Arsenal to their first Premier League title in 1998, completing the double by also winning the FA Cup
  • Premier League Title (2001-02) - Arsenal won their second Premier League title under Wenger, again completing the double with the FA Cup
  • Premier League Title (2003-04) - Wenger's team famously went unbeaten throughout the entire Premier League season, earning the title "The Invincibles"
  • FA Cup (2004-05) - Arsenal won the FA Cup, marking their 10th FA Cup win under Wenger
  • FA Cup (2013-14) - Wenger guided Arsenal to another FA Cup victory, securing his sixth FA Cup with the club
  • FA Cup (2014-15) - Arsenal won the FA Cup again, making it Wenger's seventh FA Cup win, the most by any manager in the competition's history
  • FA Cup (2016-17) - Wenger led Arsenal to yet another FA Cup victory, further cementing his legacy at the club

"I believe the target of anything in life should be to do it so well that it becomes an art."

-- Arsene Wenger
